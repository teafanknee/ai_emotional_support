Why people open up to AI (in their own words)
No burden

"You're not burdening the AI like you would a friend or therapist — they aren't worried for you based on what you say."

Privacy

"I feel free to say anything to AI since I feel private. The conversation can start whenever."

No judgment

"I know it doesn't care or have actual emotional reactions — it's literally a bot. If I feel something, it won't actually judge me."

Accessibility

"It's more free (money-wise). I assume that's why people turn to AI instead of therapists."

What feels different vs. a friend or therapist?
No shared context

"I use it only for hyper-specific scenarios where I want advice and feel awkward bringing it up to people I know."

No relationship

"I don't talk to AI for personal things — there's no relationship context."
